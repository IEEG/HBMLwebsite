<h1 style="text-align:center; font-style:italic;">
Low-Intensity Ultrasonic Brain Stimulation for Investigational Treatment of Refractory Fibromyalgia
</h1>

<div style="text-align:center; margin: 2rem 0;">
  <img src="/assets/images/fibromyalgia-brain.jpg"
       alt="Illustration of ultrasonic brain stimulation"
       style="max-width:500px; width:100%;">
</div>

<p style="text-align:center; font-size:1.25rem;">
We are seeking individuals interested in participating in a clinical study to evaluate
the non-invasive, low intensity ultrasonic stimulation for investigational treatment
of fibromyalgia.
</p>

<h2>If you:</h2>

<ul>
  <li>have been diagnosed with fibromyalgia</li>
  <li>are at least 22 years old</li>
  <li>have tried at least two medications but continue to have symptoms</li>
  <li>have tried non-drug treatment (i.e., physical therapy) but continue to have symptoms</li>
</ul>

<p style="font-size:1.4rem;">
<strong>You may be eligible for this study.</strong>
In this study, you will undergo a comprehensive evaluation and MRI,
and four 40-minute investigational treatment sessions.
<u>All study-related care is provided at no cost to you.</u>
</p>
